About Me

I'm a software tester passionate about uncovering hidden issues, improving quality, and ensuring users experience software the way it was meant to be. I thrive on precision and curiosity—digging deeper to ask ‘what could go wrong?’ and turning potential risks into reliable solutions. With an eye for detail and a commitment to excellence, I focus on shaping software into products that earn trust, deliver value, and stand the test of real-world use.

I believe good testing is not just about finding bugs, but about asking the right questions, exploring possibilities, and thinking like the end user.

My goal is to give teams confidence in every release by minimizing risks and making software more reliable and enjoyable for users.
